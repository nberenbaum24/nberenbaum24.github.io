Some General Info

The blobfish was originaly found in just 2003 during an expedition in New Zealand, the first one ever found, the expedition team named it "Mr. Blobby". Another interesting bit of knowledge is that blobfish's can live up to 130 years old! One more thing is that the blobfish isn't always blobby, when in it's usual habitat (deep sea), it looks like a regular fish because of the great amount of preassure that the deep sea creates.

Very Fun Facts

Did you know...

That in 2013 the blobfish was crowned the ugliest animal in the world winning in a landlide of 795 votes out of 3,000.

The blobfish doesn't have a skeleton which is why it looks like a blob.

The diet of a blobfish includes: sea urchins, shellfish, crabs, lobsters, mollusks, microscopic bacteria, and sea pens. The interesting thing about this is due to the blobfish's lack of structure they don't hunt actively. So how do they achieve such a great diet? They wait for something to pass by and they ambush it swallowing in a big gulp.
